About us

RM Man Power Ireland is an Irish based company. It is one of Ireland’s leading recruitment consultant services with over 3 years’ experience. RM Man Power Ireland Resource Limited is a trusted consultant services that is specialized in offering recruitment solutions to companies and organisations, also, providing work permit consultation and aims to accommodate clients with long-term employment, world-wide.

Statistics from the Department of Trade, Enterprise and Employment have shown that more Irish employment permits were issued in this year. If you are in need of a work permit, or would like to learn more about the application process, please email our team today at info@rmmpi.com The Department of Business, Enterprise & Innovation have published a link for all persons connected to an employment permit, issued or applied for.

Our story

Established in 2016, RM Man Power Ireland Resource Limited established itself for jobs across Ireland initially focused in vast meat factories. The company has now expanded to provide consultations, work permit and jobs globally, around the world.

RM Man Power Ireland Resource Limited reputation is based on forming long-term partnerships with the companies they aim to work with and their clients, providing them with innovative and cost effective solutions.
This understanding and knowledge is due to the experience and quality of the team. Their highly motivated consultants are guided with a strong management panel, focused and committed to what they aim for.
RM Man Power Ireland Resource Limited success is driven by the philosophy to provide high quality, professional work and service to the clients. The mission – to match the right job to the right candidate!
